Personality layer

Add signal, not noise: smart meme placement.

Your instinct to add memes is good, but only if they feel curated. They should release tension after dense theory, deepen memorability, and reinforce your point of view rather than making the work feel unserious.

Placement logic

Use humor where attention naturally drops.

Best placements: after case studies, before FAQ, or inside an "operator notes" strip. Treat memes as editorial punctuation, not a separate gimmick page.
